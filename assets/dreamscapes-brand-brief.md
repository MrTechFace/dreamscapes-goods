# Dreamscapes Goods — Brand Brief

## Brand Overview

**Brand Name:** Dreamscapes Goods  
**Website:** shop.dreamscapesgoods.com  
**Business:** Print-on-demand clothing (Printify fulfillment), custom-designed prints  
**Parent Company:** Onyxium AB

---

## Brand Philosophy

> *"In our world, anything is possible."*

Dreamscapes Goods exists at the intersection of imagination and everyday life. The name itself is a portal — a reminder that reality is stranger, richer, and more mysterious than most people dare to believe. Every piece is a quiet statement: the wearer sees the world differently.

---

## Mission

To create premium quality clothing that celebrates curiosity, the unexplained, and the extraordinary — for people who know that the mundane is just the surface of something much weirder.

---

## Target Audience

**Age:** 25–40  
**Mindset:** Curious, independent, culturally aware. Not trend-chasers — they buy less but buy better. They appreciate craft and originality. The kind of person who notices a cool, slightly strange design and says *"what is that?"*  
**Values:** Quality over quantity, authenticity, originality  
**Aesthetic sensibility:** Drawn to things that feel cool and a little off — not mainstream, not tryhard, just genuinely interesting

---

## Brand Personality

- **Cool** — effortlessly, not desperately
- **Weird** — in the best possible way (original, unexpected)
- **Premium** — quality materials, thoughtful design
- **Expansive** — the brand world has no ceiling; anything fits under Dreamscapes

**Tone of voice:** Confident, slightly mysterious, never ironic or self-deprecating. Speaks like someone who knows something you don't — but invites you in.

---

## Visual Identity

**Color palette:**
- Background: Charcoal / deep black
- Text: Cream / off-white
- Accent: Neon green (#39FF14)
- Storm & atmosphere: Dark moody tones

**Logo:** Bold "DS" in neon green, wordmark "Dreamscapes Goods" in baseball script (white/cream)

**Aesthetic:** Dark, atmospheric, slightly cryptic. Think stormy skies parting to reveal something extraordinary.

---

## Product Lines

**Current:**
- **Cryptid Line** — Cryptozoology-themed prints (Bigfoot, Jersey Devil, etc.)
- **General Collection** — Broader lifestyle designs united by the Dreamscapes worldview

**Philosophy:** The product range is intentionally expansive — anything that fits the feeling of "the world is stranger and more beautiful than you think" belongs here.

**Fulfillment:** Printify (print-on-demand)  
**Price point:** Premium — customers pay more for quality

---

## Tagline

**"Beyond the ordinary."**

*Alternatives:*
- *"The world is stranger than you think."*
- *"Wear the dream."*
- *"Anything is possible."*
- *"From the edges of imagination."*

---

## What Makes Dreamscapes Different

- Designs that spark conversation ("what is that?")
- Premium quality in a sea of fast fashion
- A brand world with no limits — cryptids, philosophy, the unexplained, the extraordinary
- Independent, original — not chasing trends
